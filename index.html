"Faith in Every Footstep"

Camp Overview

Join us for a spiritually enriching journey following in the footsteps of pioneers at the historic Martin's Cove. This Young Men trail & camp experience combines adventure, reflection, and faith-building experiences in the beautiful Wyoming wilderness.

What to Pack

👕 Clothing
  • Modest clothing (pants, sleeves)
  • Comfortable hiking shoes/boots
  • Hat or bandana
  • Warm layers (it can get cold at night)
  • Rain poncho or jacket
  • Long pants and Long Sleeves
🎒 Essential Gear
  • Sleeping bag and sleeping pad
  • Refillable water bottle (2 recommended)
  • Personal hygiene items
  • Headlamp/flashlight
  • Scriptures
  • Small daypack
🌟 Optional Items
  • Sunglasses
  • Gloves
  • Bug spray
  • Sunscreen
  • Trail snacks

Our Spiritual Focus

"It will all be worth it, if we will keep the faith"

This trek is more than a historical reenactment—it's a journey of faith, sacrifice, and spiritual growth. As we walk where pioneers walked, we'll discover how their faith and determination can inspire our own journey of discipleship and service.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where is the medical release form?

Please download, fill out, and save the form using the link below. Once completed, email it to: nchadbar@gmail.com

📄 Open Medical Release Form

Can my child bring a phone? 📱
Phones can be brought, but will be limited in use to camera only The focus is on spiritual growth, bonding, and reflection without digital distractions.
What about weather and emergencies? 🌦️
We monitor weather closely and have emergency protocols in place. All participants should be prepared for rapid weather changes and cold nights due to elevation.
How do we handle food allergies? 🍽️
Please make a note of that on the Medical Release Form.
What's the cell service like? 📶
Cell service at Martin's Cove is limited and unreliable. Emergency communications will be handled through trek leadership.
